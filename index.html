Uncompromising Security

How Ledger Protects Your Assets

Ledger hardware wallets are built with security as the primary focus. Unlike software wallets that are vulnerable to online threats, Ledger devices keep your private keys isolated in a secure chip, completely separate from your internet-connected computer or smartphone.

Our proprietary operating system, BOLOS, ensures that each application on your Ledger device runs in complete isolation. This means that even if one app is compromised, your other assets remain completely secure.

Every transaction must be physically confirmed on the device itself, preventing malware from altering transaction details. This combination of secure element technology, isolated application environment, and physical confirmation creates an impenetrable fortress for your digital wealth.
